About Me

Black Ghost, the Durban-based DJ with a passion for bringing good vibes to every environment. I came onto the scene in 2019 and have been spinning records ever since. My speciality is not in any specific music style, but the ability to create an atmosphere that resonates with everyone. My sets range from Private school to soulful and commercial Piano. My love for music is immense and I want nothing more than to share that passion with every person who listens to my sets.
